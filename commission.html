Commission an Artwork

If you would like to commission a custom piece from Daria Avdeeva, you can work directly with the artist to create a unique painting tailored to your vision. Commissions can be based on specific themes, subjects, sizes, or colour palettes to suit your home, office, or collection.

How It Works

  1. Initial Discussion: Share your ideas, preferred size, style, and subject matter.
  2. Proposal: Receive a draft concept, estimated price, and timeline for completion.
  3. Approval: Confirm the design and provide a deposit to begin the project.
  4. Creation: The painting is carefully crafted in Daria’s studio, with progress updates provided.
  5. Delivery: Your completed artwork is delivered, ready to hang.
