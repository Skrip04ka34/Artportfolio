About the Artist

Daria Avdeeva is an oil painter based in Dubai, UAE. Daria’s artistic journey is rooted in classical oil painting techniques studied in esteemed academies across Italy, Russia, and Bulgaria. Born in Russia and raised in the UAE since 1997, she grew up immersed in a fusion of cultures that shaped her vision.

Trained in still life, portraiture, and figurative painting, her work emphasizes technical precision, composition, and emotional depth. Blending time-honored methods with a contemporary sensibility, Daria’s paintings are distinguished by vibrant palettes and a fresh perspective that bridges tradition and innovation.

Through dedicated study and curiosity, she continues to evolve—exploring new ways to push the boundaries of her craft.
